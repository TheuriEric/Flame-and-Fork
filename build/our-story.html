How It All Began

Flame & Fork was born from a simple dream - to create a place where every meal feels like home. Founded in 2018 by Chef Maria Rodriguez and her husband Antonio, our restaurant began as a small family kitchen built on recipes passed down through generations.

Today, we've grown into a space where comfort, culture, and good food come together, but we've never forgotten our roots - every dish is still prepared with the same love and attention as those first family meals.
